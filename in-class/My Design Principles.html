Ethan's Design Principles

Key Principles

  • Visibility: of the functions, instructions, etc.
  • Consistency
  • Feedback: "response" from the object
  • Affordance: an attribute of an object that allows people to know how to use it (mouse button invites pushing)

Complexity Considerations

  • There is a limit to how simple we can make an app
  • Use icons - but ensure their meaning is obvious
  • Allow users to learn by doing
  • Be as easy as email!

Cultural Considerations

  • Individualism versus communitarianism:
    • Do people beleive in freedom or is the group greater?
  • Neutral versus emotional
    • Do people place value on rules and laws? Or is the world circumstantial?
  • Universalism versus particularism:
    • Are work and personal lives kept seperate or do they overlap?
  • Achievement versus ascription:
    • Are people valued for what they do or who they are?
  • Sequential versus synchronous time:
    • Some people like events to happen in a striated sequence, othes beleive past, present, and future are interwoven
  • Internal direction versus outer direction:
    • Some cultures profess to control nature and enviroment, others believe the opposite

Conceptual Considerations

  • A mental model is the user's representaion
  • Design the conceptual model purposely
  • Be wary of a poor or erroneous model
  • Make sure the mental model and the conceptual model match
  • Metaphors need to have affrodances and constraints

Human Center Design Principles

  • Provide discoverability
  • Provide Feedback
  • Utilize a conceptual model
  • Include affordances, signifiers, constraints
  • Ensure you have good mappings

Emotional / Accessibility Principles

  • Use multiple cues
  • CLear instructions
  • For text, use TTS
  • Use Alt-text for images
