Question 1

Anthony Ortony's model of emotional design is a model with three levels: Visceral, Behavioral, and Reflective. The lowest level is Visceral which are parts of the brain that are prewired to respond automatically to events happening in the physical world. The next level is Behavioral, which is brain processes that control everyday behavior. The highest level is the Reflective level which is when the brain processes involve contemplating.
